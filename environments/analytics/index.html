Overview

Every team will get their own Analytics Environment, which they will use to analyze data with Spark. Your Analytics Environment is comprised of the following cloud services:

  • Upload/Download service - Get files from your personal computers to the cloud
  • Spark Virtual Machine - Run spark jobs

Requesting your Environment

Each team will get a single Analytics Environment. Team leaders should request the environment here.

Using your Environment

Accessing your Spark VM

  1. As the Team Leader log in to the IM Demo Cloud and go to your project page.
  2. Follow the instructions to SSH into your Spark VM.
  3. From within your Spark VM you can run shell commands such as pyspark, spark-shell, and hdfs

Uploading code and data to your Spark VM

  1. As the Team Leader log in to the IM Demo Cloud and go to your project page.
  2. Click the "Uploads/Downloads" link, this will open up a new tab in your browser
  3. Upload your files to your Spark server using the Upload/Download page.
  4. As soon as the process completes, you should be able to access your files on your Spark VM in the /uploads directory.

Alternatively you can use the wget command from within your Spark VM to get files from external websites or servers.

Downloading files from your Spark VM to your personal computer

  1. From the Spark VM, move any files you want to down to the /uploads directory.
  2. As the Team Leader log in to the IM Demo Cloud and go to your project page.
  3. Click the "Uploads/Downloads" link, this will open up a new tab in your browser
  4. You should see the files you moved from Step 1 in the file listing. Simply click the file to download.
